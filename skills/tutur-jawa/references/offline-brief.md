# Offline Brief

## Local Facts

- Purpose: support cautious drafting and review for Bahasa Jawa.
- Region/context: Jawa Tengah, DI Yogyakarta, Jawa Timur, diaspora Jawa, and local varieties.
- ISO 639-3: `jav`.
- Glottocode: `java1254`.
- Source confidence: Level 2 because public dictionary and grammar references exist.
- Everyday Bahasa Jawa is separate from Keraton Solo Kedhaton and Keraton Jogja Bagongan.

## Guardrails

- Do not treat "Jawa" as one neutral style.
- Ask whether the target is ngoko, krama, krama inggil, public announcement style, or a local dialect.
- Do not use ngoko for elders, formal events, public notices, or respectful requests unless requested.
- Do not mix court-register Kedhaton/Bagongan into ordinary Bahasa Jawa unless requested.
- For ritual, legal, educational, official, or ceremonial use, recommend speaker/expert review.

## Sample Prompts

- "Use $tutur-jawa to make this announcement polite."
- "Check whether this sentence is too ngoko for an invitation."
- "Rewrite this for a general public Javanese audience."

## Safe Output Patterns

- Ask for target audience and speech level before sensitive drafting.
- For review, flag ngoko, krama, and mixed-level risks.
- For local varieties such as Banyumasan, Suroboyoan, Osing, Tengger, or Cirebonan, ask for the local target first.

## Local Example

Request:

> Tolong ubah ke bahasa Jawa.

Safe response shape:

> Tanyakan dulu: untuk teman sebaya, orang tua, pengumuman umum, atau acara resmi? Bahasa Jawa perlu unggah-ungguh yang sesuai.
