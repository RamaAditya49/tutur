# Lexicon

This is a cautious starter list, not a complete dictionary.

## Bagongan Markers

- `manira`: first-person pronoun in Yogyakarta Bagongan contexts.
- `pakenira`: second-person pronoun in Yogyakarta Bagongan contexts.
- `enggeh`: yes.
- `wenten`: there is / exists.
- `wikana`: do not know.

## Useful Common Forms

- `nyuwun`: request/ask.
- `matur`: say/convey.
- `rawuh`: come/arrive.
- `kersa`: willing/wish.
- `dhateng`: to/at.
- `ingkang`: which/who/that.
- `sampun`: already.
- `dereng`: not yet.
- `wilujeng`: safe/well.

## Avoid Inventing

Do not invent a Bagongan equivalent for every Indonesian word. If the right term is unknown:

- Keep the Indonesian term.
- Use a common krama equivalent.
- Add a short note that the term may need expert review.
