# Local Mirror

## Source Mirror

- Language/register: Bahasa Sunda.
- Region/context: Jawa Barat, Banten, and Sundanese communities elsewhere.
- Broad family context: Austronesian / Malayo-Polynesian.
- Glottolog: Sundanese, glottocode `sund1252`.
- ISO 639-3: `sun`.
- Badan Bahasa peta bahasa material covers Jawa Barat language mapping.
- Public references include Sundanese grammar and dictionary resources.
- The skill must account for `undak-usuk basa` or speech-level sensitivity.

## Local Usability

Use this file before browsing. The skill can produce ordinary Sundanese output, review speech-level consistency, and create short public messages when the requested level is clear.

For speeches to elders, schools, government, ceremonial events, or dialect-specific public use, mark any uncertain level or word choice for competent speaker review.

## Vocabulary And Semantics

- Speech level matters. Common target labels include everyday/loma, polite/lemes, very respectful, public announcement, and local dialect.
- Do not mix loma and lemes randomly. A single sentence can feel wrong even when each word is valid.
- Pronouns and address terms are sensitive; avoid direct pronouns when the relationship is unclear.
- Keep Indonesian technical, legal, medical, and institutional terms unless a accepted Sundanese equivalent is provided.
- Do not treat Jakarta slang or Indonesian regional accent as Bahasa Sunda.

## Generation Rules

1. Identify speech level before writing: loma, lemes, public polite, youth/casual, or dialect-specific.
2. If speech level is missing but the target is public, choose restrained polite language and mark uncertainty.
3. Preserve names, titles, place names, dates, and facts.
4. Avoid high-respect forms unless the relationship requires them.
5. For sensitive uses, provide a review note after the output.

## Starter Lexicon Policy

Do not bulk-copy dictionaries. Add only verified terms and speech-level pairs when the level is clear. Prefer examples that show how level choice affects the whole sentence.
