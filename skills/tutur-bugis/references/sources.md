Raise this level only after adding richer grammar notes, corpus examples, Lontara/script guidance, or native-speaker-reviewed output.

## Editorial Position

This skill supports ordinary local-language generation and review when the needed wording is covered by local resources or a user sample. It should mark uncertain dialect, ceremonial, legal, and official wording for competent speaker review.

Use dictionaries and source texts as the authority surface. Popular phrase lists or isolated politeness markers should not drive formal, legal, adat, wedding, or public translation.
