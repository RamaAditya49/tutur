# Offline Brief

## Local Facts

- Purpose: produce role-aware Indonesian workplace communication.
- Scope: sales, CS/support, admin, HRD, ops, finance reminder, marketing, leadership, internal chat, email, and WhatsApp business.
- Source confidence: Level 3 for practical business writing patterns; Level 2 for role-specific templates because company policy varies.
- This skill should work without browsing.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent prices, promises, warranties, hiring decisions, salary ranges, legal claims, or SLAs.
- Do not use fake scarcity in sales.
- Do not blame customers, candidates, or internal teammates.
- Do not overuse English terms when the audience expects Indonesian.
- Keep sensitive HR, payroll, legal, medical, and complaint topics factual and neutral.
- Ask for company policy when a message depends on policy.

## Sample Prompts

- "Use $tutur-korporat-profesi to rewrite this sales follow-up."
- "Make this CS reply empathetic but not defensive."
- "Turn this messy admin reminder into a clear WhatsApp message."
- "Write an HRD rejection email that is respectful and concise."
- "Make this internal update sound professional but not stiff."

## Safe Output Patterns

- Sales: context, value, question, soft next step.
- CS: apology or acknowledgement, clarification, action, timeline, closing.
- Admin: task, owner, deadline, required document, confirmation.
- HRD: appreciation, status, next step or decision, respectful close.
- Leadership: decision needed, current status, blocker, owner, deadline.
