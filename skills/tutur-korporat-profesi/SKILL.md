# Tutur Korporat Profesi

## Overview

Use this skill to write Indonesian professional communication that matches the role, channel, audience, and business goal. It covers sales, CS/support, admin/ops, HRD/recruitment, finance reminders, marketing, leadership, and general corporate chat.

Default to clear, concise Indonesian. Use English corporate terms only when the audience naturally uses them.

## Workflow

1. Identify the role and objective.
   - Sales: prospect, follow up, qualify, handle objection, close softly.
   - CS/support: acknowledge, empathize, clarify, solve, set timeline, close.
   - Admin/ops: coordinate tasks, documents, schedule, reminders, status.
   - HRD/recruitment: invite, reject, offer, update, explain process, policy.
   - Finance/admin billing: remind, confirm payment, clarify invoice, escalate.
   - Leadership/corporate: update, align, decide, unblock, summarize.

2. Identify channel and audience.
   - WhatsApp: shorter, warmer, direct.
   - Email: structured, subject-aware, complete.
   - Internal chat: concise, task-first, low ceremony.
   - Public-facing: standard Indonesian, no risky claims.

3. Choose register.
   - Formal: official, complete, no slang.
   - Professional: clear, respectful, efficient.
   - Startup/corporate casual: direct, light code-mix if natural.
   - Sales/CS warm: friendly without fake intimacy.

4. Compose.
   - Lead with context.
   - State the ask, issue, or next step.
   - Include owner, deadline, and dependency when relevant.
   - Avoid empty corporate jargon and unsupported claims.

## Output Style

- For direct writing requests, provide the message first.
- For review requests, list role/tone risks, then give a better version.
- For multiple roles, provide labeled variants.
- Do not invent policy, prices, legal terms, salary promises, discounts, SLAs, or product claims.
