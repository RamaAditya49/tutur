# Sources

Use these sources as the initial research base. This skill is intentionally conservative because living palace registers are culturally specific and not fully captured by short web references.

## Core Source

- UGM Program Studi Bahasa, Sastra, dan Budaya Jawa: "Seputar Jawa: Basa Kedhaton dan Basa Bagongan, Ragam Tutur Khas Keraton Surakarta dan Yogyakarta"
  - https://sastrajawa.fib.ugm.ac.id/seputar-jawa-basa-kedhaton-dan-basa-bagongan-ragam-tutur-khas-keraton-surakarta-dan-yogyakarta/
  - Key use: distinguishes Surakarta `Basa Kedhaton` from Yogyakarta `Basa Bagongan`; notes special pronouns, grammar, and limited special vocabulary.

## Court Context

- Mangkunegaran official site: https://mangkunegaran.id/about-us
  - Key use: confirms Mangkunegaran as a living Surakarta court/cultural institution, founded in 1757.
  - Do not treat Mangkunegaran as automatically identical to Kasunanan Surakarta.

## General Language References

- KBBI Daring: https://kbbi.kemdikbud.go.id/
- EYD V: https://ejaan.kemdikbud.go.id/

Use KBBI/EYD only for Indonesian support text and explanatory notes. They are not enough to validate Basa Kedhaton output.

## Editorial Position

This skill can create respectful, court-inspired outputs. It should not claim to produce authoritative palace protocol, ritual speech, or historically exact manuscript language.
