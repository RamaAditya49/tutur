# Examples

## Target-Language Examples

Surakarta-leaning court-inspired:

> Kula nyuwun idin ngaturaken pawartos bilih adicara badhe kawiwitan tabuh wolu dalu.

Meaning:

> Kami mohon izin menyampaikan kabar bahwa acara akan dimulai pukul delapan malam.

Respectful blessing:

> Mugi kulawarga ageng tansah pinaringan wilujeng saha katentreman.

Meaning:

> Semoga keluarga besar selalu diberi keselamatan dan ketenteraman.

Note:

These are working court-inspired examples, not verified palace protocol.

## Light Court Flavor

Input:

> Kami mohon izin menyampaikan kabar bahwa acara akan dimulai pukul delapan malam.

Output:

> Kula nyuwun idin ngaturaken pawartos bilih adicara badhe kawiwitan tabuh wolu dalu.

Note:

This is a respectful Javanese output with Surakarta-leaning court flavor, not a verified palace protocol formula.

## More Formal Output

Input:

> Semoga keluarga besar selalu diberi keselamatan dan ketenteraman.

Output:

> Mugi kulawarga ageng tansah pinaringan wilujeng saha katentreman.

## Avoiding Unclear Pronouns

Input:

> Saya akan datang jika Anda menghendaki.

Safer output:

> Menawi kakersakaken, rawuh badhe katindakaken.

Why:

The sentence avoids choosing a role-sensitive first-person and second-person pair when speaker status is unclear.
