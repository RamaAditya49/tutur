# Lexicon

This is a cautious starter list, not a complete dictionary.

## Known Kedhaton Markers

- `mara`: first-person pronoun in some Surakarta Kedhaton contexts.
- `para`: second-person pronoun in some Surakarta Kedhaton contexts.
- `manira`: first-person pronoun in some court contexts.
- `pakenira`: second-person pronoun in some court contexts.
- `jengandika`: second-person respectful form in some Surakarta contexts.
- `enggeh`: yes.
- `wenten`: there is / exists.
- `wikana`: do not know.
- `sinerat`: written.
- `lumebet`: enter.
- `mangangge`: wear/use.

## Use Common Krama Carefully

Use common krama forms when they fit:

- `nyuwun`: request/ask.
- `matur`: say/convey.
- `rawuh`: come/arrive.
- `kersa`: willing/wish.
- `dhateng`: to/at.
- `ingkang`: which/who/that.
- `sampun`: already.
- `dereng`: not yet.

## Avoid Inventing

Do not invent a court equivalent for every Indonesian word. If the right term is unknown:

- Keep the Indonesian term.
- Use a common krama equivalent.
- Add a short note that the term may need expert review.
