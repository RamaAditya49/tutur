# Examples

## Cautious Drafting

Input:

> Buat ajakan pendek untuk menjaga bahasa Minangkabau.

Safer response shape:

1. Ask whether the target is general Minang, local dialect, or adat context.
2. Draft a simple Indonesian baseline.
3. Use verified Minangkabau wording where references support it.

## Review Mode

Input:

> Cek apakah teks ini terasa Minang atau cuma Indonesia yang diberi partikel.

Response shape:

1. Flag Indonesian structure with superficial Minang markers.
2. Check important terms against dictionary/grammar sources.
3. Recommend native-speaker review for public publication.

## Adat Caution

For adat, title, kinship, or ceremonial language, do not improvise. Ask for source phrases or recommend review by a knowledgeable Minangkabau speaker.
